About

Peace Bmx Co started in approximately 2015 by two people, Dave and Chris. After a countless time they made the brand big just from social media (Instagram). When a fanbase was built up they held their first contest to decide who'd be team riders. This of which was decided through posted clips and edits of the rider. Time passed and they picked winners who were the most passionate not only about the sport to the potential team they'd be on.
